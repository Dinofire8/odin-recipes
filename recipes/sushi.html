Lasgna Recipe

Lasagna is a layered Italian dish made with wide, flat pasta sheets,

typically interleaved with sauces like tomato or meat-based ragu and cheese

then baked until bubbly and golden


Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Meat Sauce
  • Bechamel Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings

Directions

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  2. Make Bechamel Sauce
  3. Cook Lasagna Noodles
  4. Preheat Oven to 375 Degrees
  5. Assemble Lasagna
  6. Bake for 25-30 Minutes
  7. Rest and Serve
