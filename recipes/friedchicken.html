Fried Chicken Recipe

Fried Chicken is a dish where chicken pieces are seasoned, coated in flour, and deep fried

until crispy and golden


Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Coating
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Directions

  1. Marinate Chicken
  2. Prepare Coating
  3. Coat Chicken
  4. Heat Oil
  5. Fry Chicken
  6. Drain
  7. Serve
